Favorite Restaurant: The Palms

I like The Palms partly due to their menu consisting of unique food choices such as blue cheese burgers, and fried pickles. Another reason I like the restaurant is because the portion sizes are massive and most the time I take home leftovers for a decent price. The last reason is I like the palms is that it isn't far from the college so I if I go out to eat with friends from college I don't have to drive far.
